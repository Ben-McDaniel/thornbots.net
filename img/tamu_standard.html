Meet the robots

 

Hero

Launches 42mm projectiles, one of the strongest robots with high damage output.

 

Standard

Launches 17mm projectiles and completes on field tasks that activate boosts.

 

Engineer

Delivers projectiles, collects team currency and resurrects teammates.

 

Sentry

The Sentry must function autonomously.
It moves along the Sentry Rail and defends the base with 17mm projectiles.

 

Aerial

Attacks from the air and provides a birds eye view for the entire team.

 

Dart

The dart launcher must hit stationary targets throughout the match for critical damage
